Reading

Hi, I’m Maryphin, and my greatest hobby is reading. I’ve been an avid reader from a young age, with romance as my favorite genre. I love getting lost in stories—watching characters grow, connect, and fall in love through witty banter and undeniable chemistry.

I also enjoy suspense and thrillers that keep me on edge, always guessing what will happen next. Reading has helped me grow not just emotionally, but intellectually as well.

Recently, I’ve been exploring more non-fiction, and the experience has been eye-opening. It’s fascinating how carefully chosen words can uplift and share knowledge.
